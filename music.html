From September 2024 until June 2025 I followed music composition lessons which was an amazing experience! I learned a TON! However, I know there's even more I have yet to learn. We mainly focussed on classical music theory so just major and minor scales. In May we had to make a portfolio containing some of our music pieces that we were content with. Most of mine are quite short, but hopefully you'll still find them delightful. The piece for guitar is a complete piece that I hope to be able to play myself sometime. For now these are all simulations made with Musescore. Have fun!
